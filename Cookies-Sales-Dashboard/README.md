Project Objectives

Analyse cookie sales performance across different countries and years.
Track total revenue generated and total cookies shipped.
Identify the top customers contributing to overall sales revenue.
Compare yearly revenue growth trends to evaluate business performance.
Build an interactive dashboard for monitoring cookie sales metrics efficiently.
Data Cleaning & Preparation


The following preprocessing and cleaning steps were carried out:

Removed duplicate and inconsistent entries from the dataset.
Handled missing values to improve data quality.
Standardised country and customer names.
Converted revenue and shipment columns into appropriate numeric formats.
Created DAX measures for:
Total Revenue
Total Cookies Shipped
Revenue by Country
Revenue by Year
Applied formatting and filtering to improve dashboard readability and interaction.
Key Insights
The United States generated the highest revenue among all countries.
Revenue showed noticeable growth from 2024 to 2025.
A small group of customers contributed a significant percentage of total revenue.
Cookie shipment volume remained strong alongside revenue growth.
Revenue trends suggest increasing market demand over time.


Recommendations

Strengthen customer retention strategies for top-performing customers.
Increase market expansion efforts in countries with strong revenue potential.
Use yearly trend analysis to forecast future sales and inventory demand.
Develop targeted promotions in lower-performing regions to boost sales.
Continue using dashboard analytics to support strategic business decisions and sales monitoring.
